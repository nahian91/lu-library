About RKC Central Library

The RKC Central Library is the main library of Leading University. It has been named as Rabeya Khatun Chowdhury Central Library (RKCCL) to commemorate the memory of the Co-founder of LU Late Rabeya Khatun Chowdhury. The library is located on the first floor of the second academic building.

The library is fully automated with the assistance of KOHA (ILMS) and equipped with books, national and international academic journals, academic reports, theses, dissertations, projects, internships, e-books and e-journals. The library remains open from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm except national holidays.
